01 About

Crafting experiences at the intersection of function and form.

I'm a design engineer based in Berlin, with over a decade of experience crafting digital products that respect both human needs and aesthetic principles.

My approach is rooted in industrial design thinking — the belief that every element must earn its place, that form follows function, and that good design is honest. It doesn't manipulate or deceive.

Previously, I've worked with Braun, Apple, IDEO, and Teenage Engineering, bringing the rigor of physical product design to digital experiences.

03 Philosophy

Ten principles that guide every decision I make.

Good design is honest. It does not make a product more innovative, powerful or valuable than it really is. It does not attempt to manipulate the consumer with promises that cannot be kept.


Good design is long-lasting. It avoids being fashionable and therefore never appears antiquated. Unlike fashionable design, it lasts many years — even in today's throwaway society.
