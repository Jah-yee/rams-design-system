Researcher · Builder · Originator

I believe in the power of first-principles thinking—breaking down complex problems to their fundamental truths and building up from there. My work spans quantitative systems, web technologies, and the emerging landscape of AI.

Currently engaged with Spark Lab, a Shanghai-based accelerator cultivating young founders (18-25) through a dual-city immersion spanning Shanghai and Silicon Valley. We operate as "co-builders first, investors second."

The questions that drive me: How do complex systems emerge from simple rules? What does it mean to build something that matters? How can technology amplify human potential rather than diminish it?

Design Philosophy
Less, but better

Inspired by Dieter Rams' ten principles of good design. Technology should be invisible—felt but not seen. The best interface is no interface at all.

"Good design is as little design as possible. Less, but better—because it concentrates on the essential aspects, and the products are not burdened with non-essentials."
— Dieter Rams
